Web Development

Web Developemnt introduced me to programming and more importantly taught me how to hone my skills to solve complex problems in simple ways, in a given timeframe.

How It All Started

I started my career as a Java developer, but I soon clawed my way out of it as Front End development is what excited me the most. Having worked on HTML, CSS and jQuery, I picked up and contributed to BackboneJS and AngularJS (1.x) projects. DurandalJS and KnockoutJS introduced me to declarative bindings, dependency management and templating. Soon after, the React-Redux boom happened and its been smooth sail ever since. I sparingly used VueJS-VueX on my own projects.

Apart from UI frameworks and libraries, I worked on a bunch of tools around the ecosystem - Gulp, Grunt, Webpack, Babel, ES6, TypeScript, PWA, Service Workers, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB (MERN), WebAssembly, React Native, Expo, Google Firebase (RealtimeDB, Cloudstore, Functions, Hosting).

You can visit my older portfolio website (no longer maintained) that I had created specifically for Front End Development.
