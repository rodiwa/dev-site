Blockchain, AI, ML

In my early days, ever since I used Napster and LimeWire, I've always been fascinated by distributed systems. My curiosity was piqued again recently when the world went running after crypto. I love to find new books to read via Reddit forums, and watch latest developments on YouTube and can even spend weekends on 16-hour Blockchain tutorials from freeCodeCamp.

Having picked up on the basics of Blockchain, AI, ML, I realise how these technologies together can be used to solve problems in various domains. I continue to pursue these only as a technical achievement for now. But I'm excited at the prospect of learning something new in the process.

Here's a few resources that helped me get started,

Machine Learning

  • Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow - Aurélien Géron
  • Introduction to Machine Learning with Python - Andreas C. Müller
  • Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning - Christopher Bishop

Blockchain

  • Internet Of Money - Vol 1, 2, 3 - Andreas Antonopoulus (or see his YouTube videos)
  • The Bitcoin Standard - Saifedean Ammus
  • Mastering Bitcoin - Andreas Antonopoulus
  • Mastering Ethereum - Andreas Antonopoulus
  • Solidity, Blockchain, and Smart Contract Course - FreeCodeCamp (YouTube Video)
  • MIT OpenCourseWare - Blockchain and Money (YouTube PlayList)

Artificial Intelligence

  • Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach - Peter Norvig
  • MIT OpenCourseWare - Artificial Intelligence (YouTube PlayList)
  • Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning - Christopher Bishop
