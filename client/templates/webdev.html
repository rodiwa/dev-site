Web Development

Web Developemnt introduced me to programming and more importantly taught me how to hone skills to solve complex problems in simple, efficient ways in a given timeframe.

How It All Started

I started my career as a Java developer, but I soon clawed my way out of it as Front End development is what excited me the most. It started with HTML, CSS and jQuery, BackboneJS and AngularJS (1.x) projects at work. DurandalJS, KnockoutJS introduced me to declarative bindings, dependency management and templating. Soon, the React-Redux boom happened and its been a smooth sail ever since.

I've also worked with Gulp, Grunt, Webpack, Babel, ES6, TypeScript, PWA, Service Workers, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB (MERN), React Native, Expo, Google Firebase (RealtimeDB, Cloudstore, Functions, Hosting).
